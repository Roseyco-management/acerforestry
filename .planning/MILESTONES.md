# Project Milestones: Acer Forestry Website

## v1.0 MVP (Shipped: 2026-02-08)

**Delivered:** Professional marketing website for Scottish forestry contractor with three targeted conversion funnels, modern technical foundation, and production deployment.

**Phases completed:** 1-10 (22 plans total)

**Key accomplishments:**

- Modern Technical Foundation - Established Next.js 15 with TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and quality tooling (ESLint, Prettier) providing fast, SEO-friendly static generation
- Professional Scottish Forestry Brand - Designed cohesive visual identity with refined dark green (#1B4332) AF monogram logo, natural color palette, and Inter typography system blending professionalism with natural warmth
- Three Targeted Conversion Funnels - Built dedicated pages for Forest Managers (HSE compliance, survival rates, expertise), Services showcase (7 service offerings), and Subcontractor recruitment (weekly pay, organized sites, competitive rates)
- Polished Animations & Interactions - Integrated Framer Motion with professional entrance animations and scroll effects maintaining understated aesthetic appropriate for forestry industry
- Comprehensive SEO & Performance - Implemented complete metadata system, LocalBusiness structured data, OpenGraph social sharing, and optimization for rural Scotland's slower connections
- Production Launch - Successfully deployed to Vercel with analytics integration (GA4, Clarity, Meta Pixel), cross-browser/device testing, and soft launch strategy for monitoring and content enhancement
